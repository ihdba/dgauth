Stategy

This new opportunity is the key to their desire and it’s only attainable through my new mechanism. That’s your mission. Write it down.
As you can see, you can break the one belief down into three main elements: the new opportunity, your prospect’s desire, and the new mechanism. Let’s dig deeper into each one.
The new opportunity is what makes your idea unique, your unique selling proposition (USP). If you don’t have one, your idea is worthless.

A fresh chance, a brand-new possibility of success where only disappointment has resulted before.

The new opportunity reveals WHAT is unique about your solution, the new mechanism reveals HOW it works.

Once you know your new opportunity, your customer’s desire, and your new mechanism, you’re ready to write down your one belief.
