Stategy

This new opportunity is the key to their desire and it’s only attainable through my new mechanism. That’s your mission. Write it down.

Ultimate Goal of Deli Greece is to increase the amount of producers and visitors signed and to keep high percentage of visitors on email list. It is to attract the interest of more producers, not only farmers but also of those of smaller or bigger workshops in order to promote their products. The ultimate goal is the marketing promotion and consulting of the brand. (Brand can be anything form beans and honey to t-shirts and shows and hotels.) How do we see the product? The product have to be presented on the page in a attractive way, with key words describing the product and prossibility for further information. The price and purchasing details must be clear and consise. Contact information of the producer (only registered producers can publish their products) must be detailed and clear.

This new opportunity is the key to their desire and it’s only attainable through my new mechanism.

What is the new opportunity?
It is the promotion of the product throught the pages of Deli Greece. What is their desire? It is to get more customers and sell more of the product. Deli Greece is providing the unique possibility of promoting their product in order to increase sales.
